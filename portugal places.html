Lisbon – The Chill but Cool Capital

Portugal’s sunny capital is full of pastel-colored buildings, cute trams, and amazing views from the hilltops. Don’t miss:

  • Belém Tower – a tower that's been standing since 1519
  • Alfama – get lost in the narrow streets
  • Time Out Market – yummiest bites 🧀🍤

Sintra – Fairytales, But Make It Real

Sintra looks like it came out of a Disney movie. Forests, palaces, and fog that makes it spooky-but-cool.

  • Palácio da Pena – rainbow-colored castle
  • Quinta da Regaleira – mystical garden 🌀

Porto – Where the River Meets the Vibe

This northern city is where you sip on Port wine, walk on cute bridges, and chill by the Douro River.

  • Dom Luís I Bridge – sunset views
  • Livraria Lello – Harry Potter vibes 🪄📚
  • Ribeira – colorful riverfront area

Algarve – Beaches That Slay Harder Than Summer

The southern coast is all cliffs and turquoise water. It’s giving dreamy vacay.

  • Benagil Cave – a beach inside a cave
  • Ponta da Piedade – dramatic cliffs
  • Lagos – beach town vibes 🏖️🌴
